About Nuvo Group

We deliver a concept-to-completion strategy, which encompasses the entire spectrum of solutions including electrical, data, fire, communications, security, smart systems and audio visual. We offer a total service offering in design, installation, project management and maintenance of commercial and industrial projects. We are a professional, service-oriented organisation and industry leaders with a track record of delivering high quality services on time and within budget.

Nuvo Group has an elite team of technical advisors and technicians that manage all facets of the project and contract, delivering a “Total Solution” in a seamless and professional manner. Where competitor companies sub-contract specialist skills into the business, Nuvo Group uses an in-house model under one project manager and one technical team, minimising the need for external specialists.
